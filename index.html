About

Rose Koenings is a developer and designer that started out with a B.A. in English from UW-Whitewater. She wrote technical documentation for luxury planes and medical equipment. In her free time she participated in hackathons. Her team went on to win 2nd place for an anxiety app and 3rd place for a game. She found great joy in being able to bring her ideas to life and she used that growing passion as the driving force behind teaching herself HTML, CSS, JavaScript and to continue learning. Soon she was able to make her first professional baby step into code working with databases.

Her writing skills pair well with her keen eye for design and commitment to producing streamlined effective web applications. In her experiences teaching English in Japan she has found camaraderie, communication and creative problem solving to be a priority in life as well as in the office. She's looking for an opportunity to join a team where she can use her skill set in a collaborative fun loving environment. You can find her in the dairy state looking for the next best Thai restaurant.

My Guest Book

My Guest Book makes responding to a wedding instantaneous and organization accessible and clean. I used HTML, CSS, and AngularJS. Enter the example names in the input boxes to navigate to the next page of the site.

  • Website: My Guest Book

Song Bird

Song Bird is an app I made to test out my knowledge of AngularJS, pagination and the itunes API. You can search for any song in itunes and view five results on each page. You can add any song you want to your playlist below. If you change your mind later you can remove any song you'd like from your playlist. You cannot add duplicate songs to your playlist.

  • Website: Song Bird
